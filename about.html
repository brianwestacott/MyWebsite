My Passions & Interests

Travel

My passion for travel began at age eight during a family trip to Germany. That first international experience ignited a curiosity that hasn’t faded, leading me to explore diverse landscapes and cultures—from Alaska to California to Puerto Rico.

Over the years, I’ve learned to manage the logistics of independent travel, building problem-solving skills and resilience. Each trip has strengthened my ability to adapt and think on my feet.

This hobby connects directly to my future goals, especially my study abroad trip to New Zealand. The adaptability and global awareness I gain will translate directly into my accounting career.
