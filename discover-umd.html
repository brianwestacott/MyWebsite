Why I Chose UMD

Location

UMD offers a unique mix of strong academics and access to outdoor activities along Lake Superior.

Hockey

The energy at UMD hockey games creates a strong sense of community and school pride.

Academics

The Labovitz School of Business provides hands-on learning and strong preparation for an accounting career.

What I Love About Duluth

Duluth combines a small-town feel with outdoor adventure. From Lake Superior to local trails and Canal Park, it’s a great place to live and study.

Campus & City Highlights

Hartley Nature Center

A great place to hike, trail run, and enjoy nature close to campus.

Canal Park

A popular area with shops, food, and views of Lake Superior.

Griggs Beach

A social spot on campus for sports and relaxing with friends.

Kathryn A. Martin Library

A quiet and productive place to study and focus on coursework.
