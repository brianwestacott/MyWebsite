About Me

Hello! My name is Brian Westacott, and I am a current student at the University of Minnesota Duluth, where I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting. Originally from Minneapolis, I’ve always had an analytical mindset; I chose to pursue accounting because I enjoy the logic of mathematics and find that the principles of financial reporting just "make sense" to me. My goal is to secure a challenging internship for the summer of 2027, successfully complete my degree, and launch a fulfilling career in the accounting field. When I’m not diving into spreadsheets, you can usually find me exploring the great outdoors. This website serves as a window into my professional journey, academic background, and the experiences that have shaped me so far, thank you for visiting!
